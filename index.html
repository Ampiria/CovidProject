COVID19: Variants of Concern

Vaccination rates in the US are promising for slowing down the spread of COVID19; however, we are also seeing a number of concerning variants that threaten to extend the lifetime of the pandemic. "Variants of concern" is a classification the CDC gives to the COVID variants that are being the most actively monitored.
